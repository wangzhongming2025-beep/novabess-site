About NovaBESS

Ready-to-use energy storage, backed by a 40-year manufacturing group.

Our Story

NovaBESS is an export brand built for distributors, installers and project buyers who need reliable, plug-and-play battery energy storage systems. We combine a lean, international-facing sales team with the production depth of one of China’s longest-established battery manufacturers.

Our partner factory has supplied industrial, motive-power and energy-storage batteries since 1982. Today it operates 7 manufacturing bases with over 3,000 employees and a total footprint of more than 600 acres, giving NovaBESS customers direct access to mature engineering, stable supply and competitive pricing.

Quality & Certifications

All NovaBESS residential and mobile units ship with third-party test reports and certifications ready for customs clearance and local market access:

  • IEC 62619 — Safety requirements for lithium cells and batteries used in industrial applications
  • UN38.3 — Lithium battery transport safety (air / sea / road)
  • CE — EU market access and conformity
  • ISO 9001 — Quality management system
  • ISO 14001 — Environmental management system
  • IATF 16949 — Automotive quality management standard

Full test reports and certificates are available to serious buyers after a brief qualification call.
