Calculator FAQ

What size home battery do I need?

For grid-tied self-consumption, size to about 60% of your daily use. Off-grid, size to 1.5x your net daily use after solar. The calculator above maps the result to a concrete NovaBESS model.

How long does a home battery take to pay back?

Typically 6–12 years depending on peak/off-peak spread and cycles/day. Higher rate gaps and daily arbitrage shorten payback.

Do I need solar to use a home battery?

No. A battery also earns from peak/off-peak arbitrage and provides backup. Solar multiplies savings but is not required.

Can I expand capacity later?

Yes. NovaBESS HomeStack modules parallel from 5 kWh up to 60 kWh, so you can start small and add later.
