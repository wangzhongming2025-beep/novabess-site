Application Case Studies

How NovaBESS systems solve real energy problems — by scenario.

These are representative deployment scenarios built from our product capabilities and field experience. Named customer references and site photos will be added here after written authorization from each partner. We never publish a client's name, location or image without permission.

⛈

Off-Grid Home Backup

Where: Grid-unstable or remote homes in Southeast Asia & Africa.

Challenge: Frequent outages, no reliable grid, diesel is costly and noisy.

Solution: HomeStack 15 kWh + 5 kW solar. Auto switch on outage, recharge by sun.

Result: Lights, fridge, fans and routers run through the night; fuel cost cut sharply.

☀

Solar Self-Consumption

Where: Homeowners in EU & AU with rooftop PV.

Challenge: Excess daytime solar is exported at a low feed-in rate; evening use still draws grid power.

Solution: HomeWall 10.2 kWh stores midday solar and releases it after sunset.

Result: 60-80% more of the home's own solar used on-site; lower evening bills.

⚙

Remote Site & Telecom

Where: Construction sites, telecom cabinets, field stations.

Challenge: No grid or unstable supply; tools and equipment must keep running.

Solution: PowerBox 16.08 kWh wheeled unit — moved where needed, no install crew.

Result: Continuous power for power tools and cabinets; quieter and cheaper than diesel.

🏠

Space-Limited Apartment

Where: Apartments and town homes with little floor space.

Challenge: Tenants want backup but cannot fit a floor unit.

Solution: HomeWall 5.1 kWh wall-mounted, slim profile, parallel-ready.

Result: Essential loads protected; clean wall install with no footprint loss.
